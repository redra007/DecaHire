How We Work

Our proven methodology from first contact to successful placement

01

Understanding Your Needs

We start with in-depth discovery conversations to understand your technical requirements, team culture, growth plans, and specific challenges. This isn't a form-filling exercise—it's a genuine dialogue between technical peers.

We discuss tech stacks, architectural patterns, team structures, and the real problems you're trying to solve. This depth of understanding ensures we're looking for the right people, not just the right keywords.

02

Intelligent Sourcing

We deploy AI-powered systems that search across multiple channels simultaneously—job boards, professional networks, GitHub, technical communities, and our extensive network. Our AI evaluates candidates across dozens of dimensions beyond resume keywords.

But technology is just the first filter. Every candidate is reviewed by our technical recruiters who add context, judgment, and human insight that AI cannot replicate.

03

Rigorous Vetting

We conduct technical conversations that assess real understanding. We review code samples with an engineer's eye. We evaluate problem-solving approaches, learning ability, and collaboration skills. We look for signals that predict long-term success.

Our screening process is thorough but respectful. Candidates appreciate conversations with people who understand their work. This creates better experiences and stronger engagement throughout the process.

04

Seamless Coordination

We manage all logistics—scheduling, communications, interview preparation, and feedback collection. You focus on evaluating candidates; we handle everything else efficiently.

Throughout the process, you get real-time updates, honest feedback, and clear insights. No surprises, no delays, no confusion.

05

Offer Support & Onboarding

When you've found the right candidate, we facilitate offer discussions with market data, clear communication, and creative problem-solving. We help both sides reach agreements that feel fair and exciting.

Our involvement continues through onboarding and beyond. We check in during the first months to ensure successful integration and address any concerns early.
